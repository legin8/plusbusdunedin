Plus Bus Dunedin

What we do

At Plus Bus we provide a "door-to-door service" to help the elderly and people who struggled using public transport to get groceries home from the supermarket.

Clients are picked up from their home in a van, the van can carry up to nine passengers at a time.
A volunteer helper will carry groceries back into your house.

Days we operate/ Cost

We operate on Tuesdays and Thursdays, the bus visits.

Day of Week Supermarket For those living in

Tuesdays morning

Thursdays morning

Pak 'n' Save South Dunedin, Anderson's Bay, St Clair

Thursday afternoon

Countdown Andersons Bay South Dunedin, Anderson's Bay and Waverley

The cost for the service depended on where the client lives in Dunedin.
$10-$14, return trip.

This is based on the distance from the home to the supermarket.

The service can be booked as a regular service or as a "one-off".
